About Me

I'm Sudhin Raj B S, a passionate self-taught developer from Kerala, India, with a deep drive to build, learn, and grow. My journey into tech began without a formal degree — I only completed my SSLC — but that didn’t stop me from diving deep into the world of computers, coding, and problem-solving.

Over the years, I’ve trained myself in front-end development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and I'm now expanding into React and full-stack technologies. I regularly help BTech students understand coding concepts, build projects from scratch, and explore how software can solve real problems. Teaching others has not only strengthened my own skills but also fueled my passion for making tech more accessible. I’m currently working towards becoming a full-time developer and eventually want to specialize in iOS development using Swift. I love Apple products and dream of building sleek, powerful apps that live on millions of iPhones someday. Outside of code, I’m a tech enthusiast who follows creators like MKBHD and Mrwhosetheboss. I’m also obsessed with typing — practicing every day on Monkeytype to reach peak speed and accuracy. I'm a big believer that consistency and grit are more powerful than any degree. This portfolio is just a small window into who I am and what I’m building. I’m always open to collaboration, learning from others, and taking on new challenges that push me forward.
